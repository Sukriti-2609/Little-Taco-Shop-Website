About LTS 🌮

LTS is our new endeavour founded almost a year ago in January 2026. Our shop was built for all the taco lovers and people who dare to try new dishes 🌮🌮🌮. We hope our shop adds a unique and interesting place to our little town.

At The Little Taco Shop, we are passionate about bringing you the most authentic and flavorful tacos in this town. Our mission is to provide a delightful dining experience with every bite.

Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
